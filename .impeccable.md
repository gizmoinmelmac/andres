## Design Context

### Users
Visitors who google Andrés or land on the URL from a business card / LinkedIn / speaker listing. Primary audience: investors, potential collaborators, press, fellow founders. They want a quick read on "who is this person?" — not a full résumé. They arrive on desktop but often on mobile.

### Brand Personality
Serial founder, product executive, AI integrator. Colombian, currently between LA / Madrid / Miami. Star Wars fan. Irreverent but credible. Three words: **classified · atmospheric · still**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark. Institutional-but-mysterious — like a DHARMA Initiative research station from LOST: official seals, hexagonal motifs, coordinates, numbered sequences, typewriter/stencil energy without being retro kitsch. Contrasted by a live 3D element (Spline robot) that makes the page feel alive and contemporary. Think: classified document meets interactive art installation.
- **Theme:** Dark (appropriate — experienced people sizing you up at night, on planes, in dim offices)
- **Reference:** 21st.dev Spline spotlight card — adapted to full-page, immersive
- **Anti-reference:** Generic SaaS founder portfolio (progress bars, timeline, headshot grid)

### Design Principles
1. **Less is signal** — show almost nothing; make the visitor lean in
2. **Atmosphere over content** — the 3D scene + scanlines + vignette do the heavy lifting; words are sparse
3. **Institutional enigma** — station labels, coordinates, hex seals; not explained, just present
4. **Amber as soul** — warm amber accent, never cyan or purple; ties to coffee/Colombia/warmth under the cold institutional shell
5. **Mobile = vertical atmospheric** — gradient flips to bottom-up so the 3D robot fills the sky and content anchors at foot
