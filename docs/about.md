## Bios

### 1-Line

### Short (100 words)

Andrés Fajardo is a product executive and serial founder with 15+ years building products across B2C, B2B, and B2B2C in Europe, the US, and Latin America. He is currently Founder & CPTO of Sesh, a fan engagement platform that raised $7M and serves 300+ artists. Previously CPO at Tribbu, CPO at Dixper (acquired by Team Heretics), Operating Partner at TheVentureCity (200+ startups mentored), and CPO at Playtomic. He co-founded Wysh (acquired by Hotusa, 2017) and holds an MBA from IE Business School and a B.Sc. in Engineering from Universidad de los Andes.

Co-Founder & CPTO at Sesh, where we're reimagining how artists connect with their audience — transforming casual listeners into diehard fans through wallet pass technology, direct lock-screen engagement, and AI-powered fan intelligence.

I leverage AI extensively to streamline operations, enhance decision-making, and scale processes across product development, marketing, and business operations — helping teams work smarter and move faster.
